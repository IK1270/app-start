Blue marlin

Blue marlins are among the largest, fastest, and most recognizable fish in the world.

Just how big is a blue marlin? Female blue marlins can grow up to 14 feet (4.3 meters) in length and can weigh more than 1,985 pounds (900 kilograms)!

Bull shark

Bull sharks are the most dangerous sharks in the world, according to many experts.

This is because they're an aggressive species of shark, and they tend to hunt in waters where people often swim: along tropical shorelines.

Electric Eel

The electric eel gets its name from its shocking abilities!

The shock of an electric eel has been known to knock a horse off its feet.
